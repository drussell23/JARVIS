AMBIENT PRESENCE

JARVIS doesn't live in a window. It lives in the edge of your screen — a subtle breathing light that materializes into a full interface when summoned, then dissolves back into nothing.

State 1 — Dormant
Just a breath of light on the edge
When you're working, JARVIS is invisible except for a faint green glow on the right edge of your screen — a 3-pixel line that gently breathes. A tiny reactor dot sits at the midpoint. That's it. Your desktop is 100% yours. No panels, no windows, no overlays.
🖥️
Your desktop — completely clear
DORMANT — say "Hey JARVIS" or hover the edge
State 2 — Summoned
Say "JARVIS" and the panel materializes
Voice command, hover the edge, or press ⌘⇧J — JARVIS slides in from the right as a glass panel. Your desktop pushes left slightly to make room (like Notification Center). The panel has depth, blur, and a subtle green accent. Dismiss by pressing ⌘⇧J, clicking outside, or saying "JARVIS, dismiss."
