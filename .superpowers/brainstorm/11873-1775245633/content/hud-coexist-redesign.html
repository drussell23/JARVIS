Recommendation: Right Sidebar Panel

This is the natural macOS pattern — Notification Center, Finder sidebar, Xcode inspectors all work this way. JARVIS occupies a fixed column on the right edge, your apps use the remaining space. Toggle with ⌘⇧J. Resizable. Stays on top but never blocks. The existing compact header, Chat/Hive tabs, and all SwiftUI components work as-is — we just change the window from a full-screen overlay to a right-anchored panel.
