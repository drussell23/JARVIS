## Pro Tips

1. **Lead with impact:** Start with what it does, not how it's built
2. **Use numbers:** Metrics grab attention (300ms, 99.5%, 91% savings)
3. **Show business value:** Cost, security, reliability matter to hiring managers
4. **Demonstrate depth:** Mention architecture, not just "built an AI thing"
5. **Be specific:** "ECAPA-TDNN" > "ML model", "Cloud Run" > "cloud"
6. **Show scale:** "Production system" > "side project"
7. **Prove it works:** "Daily use" > "demo"
